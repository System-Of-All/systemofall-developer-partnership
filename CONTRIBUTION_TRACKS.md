# Contribution Tracks

The SALL Token Partnership Program recognizes contributions in the following tracks. Contributors may participate in one or more tracks depending on expertise.



## Technical
Focus on building and maintaining ecosystem infrastructure and products.

**Examples of contributions:**
- Software development (front-end, back-end, full-stack)
- Blockchain protocol design and implementation
- AI/ML and data infrastructure
- Cybersecurity, cryptography, and audits
- DevOps, cloud, and platform infrastructure



## Research
Focus on academic, applied, and technical insights that support ecosystem decisions.

**Examples of contributions:**
- Technical whitepapers and documentation
- Governance and regulatory compliance research
- Data analysis and modeling
- Exploratory research for new ecosystem innovations



## Governance
Focus on designing frameworks, standards, and structures that guide ecosystem operations.

**Examples of contributions:**
- DAO and governance frameworks
- Tokenomics design and economic modeling
- Ecosystem policy and operational standards
- Regulatory compliance strategy and review


> Contributors may participate in **one or multiple tracks** depending on their expertise, availability, and interest.
