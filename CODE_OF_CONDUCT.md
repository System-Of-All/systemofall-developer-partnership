# Code of Conduct

The SALL Token Partnership Program maintains a **professional, ethical, and collaborative environment**. All participants are expected to adhere to the following standards to ensure a productive and respectful ecosystem.



## 1. Professionalism and Ethics
- Act with integrity, respect, and fairness in all interactions.
- Collaborate constructively with other contributors, advisors, and ecosystem stakeholders.
- Uphold high standards of honesty and accountability in all program-related activities.



## 2. Confidentiality
- Protect all non-public information, materials, and discussions shared within the program.
- Do not disclose program-related information without explicit approval from the SALL Token Partnership Program leadership.



## 3. Accurate Representation
- Avoid misrepresenting your skills, experience, role, or affiliation.
- Ensure all contributions and claims are accurate, verifiable, and aligned with the scope of your participation.



## 4. Governance and Review Compliance
- Follow established governance, review, and contribution processes for all submitted work.
- Ensure contributions comply with program-defined standards, guidelines, and technical requirements.



## 5. Public Communication
- Do not promote the token, solicit investments, or represent the ecosystem publicly without **explicit written authorization**.
- All public statements, announcements, or external communications must be approved in advance by program leadership.



## Enforcement
- Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
  - Removal from the program
  - Revocation of contributor recognition
  - Restriction or denial of token allocation based on contribution


> Participants are expected to **model professional behavior** at all times. Adherence to this Code of Conduct ensures a respectful, fair, and high-quality contribution environment for everyone in the SALL Token Partnership Program.
